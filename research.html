As you might be aware from my webpage, I work on mathematical models to develop communication networks. In the process, I learn state-of-the-art tools and innovate new methods to use them. If you enjoy the same, feel free to contact.

To Prospective MS and PhD Scholars:


If you are looking to pursue MS or PhD, I feel there are some important things that you should consider. In order to pursue research, it is important that you set your expectations right. This is not a coursework program where a degree is guaranteed after attaining certain credits. Before enrolling to any research oriented program, be clear of what is expected of you both from your supervisor as well as university requirements, if any. As supervisors, our responsibility is to support you to the best of our ability in this regard.


If you are thinking of working with me, it is advisable to come and meet me in person or at least have an online discussion. Since these are long term commitments, it is better to get to know each other. It would also benefit you to know someone you are choosing to work with.


Wish you my best!
