Research Philosophy

As you might be aware from my webpage, I work on mathematical and AI models to develop communication networks. This is somewhat at the intersection of ECE and CS. I enjoy learning new mathematical concepts and tools and use them to develop new algorithms. For research, I believe in having open technical discussions and often brainstorm ideas towards novel solutions.

To Prospective MS and PhD Scholars

If you are thinking of working with me, you are always welcome to contact me and start a conversation regarding your aspirations and plans. Since these are long term commitments, it is better to get to know each other. It would also benefit you to know someone you are choosing to work with.

Wish you my best!

Prospects on Joining me

I am actively working with my collaborators at University of Melbourne, KTH, IITKGP and IITK. Thus, with me, you get an opportunity to pursue cutting-edge research with some of the top universities in India and abroad.
